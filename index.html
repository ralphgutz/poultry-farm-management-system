Products

Poultry Care

We ensure our poultry farm is handled by capable and trusted employees from farmers to nutritionists. We believe that poultry farm requires an intensive care and love as we raise them to a stress-free environment assuring that all our goods will be of good quality.



Egg Production

We believe that our customers deserve the best of the best kind of chicken eggs.

Our egg production is well handled by capable and trusted employees to ensure all eggs will be serving on the table are fresh and has acquired proper nutrition from the chickens. Teams

Our team is divided into several teams each of having qualified and capable employees that assures our operations goes smoothly and our goods and serviced to be of good quality.



Bird Management

Our employees regularly check and assess the condition of our birds for egg production. They are trusted to record farm activities such as the managing the bird population needed for tracking the poultry farm’s status and bird conditions.



Resource Management

Checks resource status pertaining to consumption, either daily, weekly, or monthly. This is to keep track and replenish resources to ensure all resources needed is highly accessible and won’t cause any opportunity loss.



Egg Production

Conducts assessment on eggs that are produced by the birds. This is to ensure that all egg that will be sold are of good condition and quality. Moreover, daily egg produce are recorded daily to acquire a picture of egg production trend.



User Control

This team is entrusted to control users that will access the system and control any malicious attempt to access data of the poultry farm that could become a threat for the overall enterprise operations.
